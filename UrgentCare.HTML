Frequently Asked Questions

What is urgent care?

Urgent care practices strive to enhance the experience of your primary doctor. We offer extended office and weekend hours to accommodate your schedule, offer an extensive set of services to allow treatment of chronic conditions, issues you face today, or even non-life-threatening conditions. Our goal at the end of the day is to provide a standard of care that eliminates the wait-time and expense and get you back to your normal.

Can urgent care replace my primary physician?

We’re honored you like us that much and we think our staff is pretty great too but Live Urgent Care should not replace your primary physician. We want to serve you when your primary physician is unavailable or you need a service they can’t fulfill!

Is urgent care more expensive than the emergency room?

Generally, urgent care visits are considerably less expensive than an emergency room visit.

Should I go to the emergency room instead?

You should visit the emergency room for life-threatening conditions including significant chest pain, shortness of breath, passing out, and severely broken bones. Please call 911 if you are experiencing an emergency.

Do you accept my insurance?

Most likely! We accept most commercial, medical managed care, and medicare advantage and exchange plans!

Can I pay with cash and/or credit cards?

Yes, we are able to accept both cash and credit cards for payment!
