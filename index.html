About Us


CEO and Founder, Browlyn Marquez, MSN, FNP-C, APN, created Live Urgent Care in hopes to offer convenient and accessible preventive, general, and family medicine available to all.


We strive to take a personalized approach to medicine and our expert providers will work with each patient to find the source of discomfort and develop a customized treatment plan that promotes wellness and vitality, beyond the visit. Each on-staff provider holds years of knowledge and experience while still offering a warm, welcoming, and compassionate bedside manner.

Live Urgent Care offers a variety of services, including in-person visits at our Bellmawr, NJ location or convenient online visits from anywhere in the world via your personal device. Offering convenient medicine is our main goal, whether it be at our site with easy walk-ins or at home on your schedule.

We also offer rapid COVID-19 results. We provide rapid antigen and guaranteed same-day PCR results at any of our New Jersey Covid-19 testing sites or from the convenience of your own home.
